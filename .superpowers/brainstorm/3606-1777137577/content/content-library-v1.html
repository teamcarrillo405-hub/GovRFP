Construction Methodology — Phased Approach
Category: Technical / Methodology
Quality Score: 94 / 100  ·  Used in 14 proposals
Quality Score 94 / 100
Content Preview
Our phased construction methodology establishes a disciplined, sequential framework for delivering federal construction projects on schedule and within budget. Phase 1 encompasses mobilization, site preparation, utility protection, and subcontractor coordination meetings to align all parties before breaking ground. Phase 2 transitions into structural work, with milestone gate reviews confirming design-to-construction conformance and proactive identification of long-lead procurement items. Phase 3 covers interior build-out, MEP rough-in, and systems commissioning with parallel quality control inspections at each trade interface. The final phase prioritizes punch-list closure, owner training, O&M documentation delivery, and a formal turnover meeting within the contractually required substantial completion window.
